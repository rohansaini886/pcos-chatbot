PCOS MRI Image Upload Guidelines

Important Notice

This application is designed to analyze ovarian MRI scans for PCOS indicators. Regular photographs of people, objects, or non-medical images will be rejected by our validation system.

Acceptable Image Types

  • Medical MRI scans of ovaries
  • File formats: JPEG, PNG, TIFF, DICOM
  • Maximum file size: 10MB
